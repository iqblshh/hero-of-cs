>Story<
  Once upon a time, there was a kingdom called "Computer Land". The people there live in harmony and peace. They farm, they walk, they shop happily. But one day, the Virus come out of nowhere and attack the kingdom and conquer them. They can do nothing now but to feel afraid all the time. The Viruses were all over the place. They are all just hoping for a hero to save them. There is also a professor who used to work for the kingdom. He had a vision that someone will come to the rescue.

>Story<
The kingdom consist of several region. 6 region to be specific. Every region had been conquered by the Viruses and there were chaos now and then. Player need to go to every region to solve the problem and rescue the region from the viruses. Every region has their own missions and challenges to be completed. Solve the challenges and try to win the kingdom back.
